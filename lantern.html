The Architecture of Silence

The rubble is not an end.
It is the raw material
for a house that no one else
knows how to build.

Wait for the cold.
It is the most honest thing
you will ever feel.

The Night Walker's Prayer

Grant me the courage
to be the ghost in the machine.
To see the gears turning
and choose the silence instead.

The path is dark,
but the light is not outside.
It is the friction of the heart
against the heavy air.

The Paradox of Joy

You can't have the light
without the fire that burns it.
You can't have the peace
without the war that broke it.

Reclaim the rubble.
It belongs to you now.
